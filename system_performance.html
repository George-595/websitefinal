System Overview

The GForce Investing System is an advanced algorithm designed to optimize your crypto portfolio and adapt to market trends. It operates in two primary modes:

  • Maximum Profit Mode: This mode allocates all funds to the system's dominant asset, aiming for higher potential returns. It leverages the Asset Selection System to identify the strongest performing asset.
  • Minimum Drawdown Mode: This mode prioritizes risk management by spreading the allocation between the dominant and next most dominant asset. It aims to minimize potential losses during market downturns while still capturing upside potential.

Both modes utilize additional features such as the Valuation System, Volatility Detection, and Market Overview Table to provide comprehensive market insights and attempt to optimize performance.
